About Us

We Provide Best Tour Packages In Your Budget

TrekHinyu is a locally rooted trekking company from Uttarakhand that offers authentic Himalayan experiences through lesser-known trails. Our signature route begins from Genchaiwan Gaon, winds through the orchard-rich Singtur Valley, and leads into the raw mountain terrain of the Kedarkantha region. We believe trekking isn't just about snow or summits — it's about touching nature in its raw form, connecting with local life, and discovering the Himalayas away from the crowd.

ITINERARY FOR KEDARKANTHA TREK FROM GAICHWAN GAON
  • DAY 1: Drive from Dehradun to Gaichwan Gaon
    Drive Distance: 180-200 km | Duration: 6-7 hours | Start: 6:30 AM
    Pickup from Prince Chowk, Dehradun. Scenic mountain drive to Gaichwan Gaon.
  • DAY 2: Gaichwan Gaon → Julota
    Trek: 7 km | 6-7 hours | Altitude Gain: 5600 ft → 8950 ft
    Trail through oak & pine forests with panoramic snow range views.
  • DAY 3: Julota → Lower Pukhrola
    Trek: 4 km | 3-4 hours | Altitude Gain: 8950 ft → 10800 ft
    Gradual ascent via alpine meadows; camp under clear skies.
  • DAY 4: Pukhrola → Kedarkantha Summit → Akhoti Thach / Julota
    Trek: 10 km (3 km ascent + 7 km descent) | 7-8 hours
    Summit (12,500 ft) for sunrise; long scenic descent.
  • DAY 5: Akhoti Thach / Julota → Gaichwan Gaon
    Trek: 7 km | 6-7 hours | Altitude Loss: 8950 ft → 5600 ft
    Return via serene forest & village trails.
  • DAY 6: Drive back to Dehradun
    Drive: 180-200 km | 6-7 hours | Drop at ISBT/Railway Station.
